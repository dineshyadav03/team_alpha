Science Park - Project Schedule Overview

This document provides an overview of the key phases and milestones for the Science Park project in Navi Mumbai.

- **Phase 1: Planning & Design (Months 1-6)**
  - Concept development and client workshops.
  - Detailed architectural and landscape design.
  - Regulatory approvals and clearances.
  - Tender documentation preparation.

- **Phase 2: Pre-Construction (Months 7-9)**
  - Contractor selection and appointment.
  - Site mobilization and groundwork.
  - Procurement of long-lead items.

- **Phase 3: Construction (Months 10-24)**
  - Structural erection and building envelope completion.
  - MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing) installation.
  - Interior fit-out and landscaping works.

- **Phase 4: Commissioning & Handover (Months 25-27)**
  - System testing and commissioning.
  - Final inspections and defect rectification.
  - Client training and facility handover.

- **Key Milestones:**
  - Design Approval: Month 5
  - Construction Start: Month 10
  - Structure Completion: Month 18
  - Project Handover: Month 27
