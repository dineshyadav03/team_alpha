NMMC Headquarters - Architectural Floor Plan Details

This document outlines the detailed floor plan specifications for the Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation (NMMC) Headquarters.

- **Ground Floor:**
  - Main lobby with reception and waiting area.
  - Public facilitation center with inquiry desks.
  - Security control room.
  - Parking access points.
  - Service areas (electrical, plumbing).

- **Typical Office Floor (Levels 1-5):**
  - Open-plan office spaces for various departments.
  - Enclosed cabins for department heads.
  - Meeting rooms of various capacities (4-seater, 8-seater).
  - Pantry and break areas on each floor.
  - Restrooms and utility closets.

- **Special Features:**
  - Central atrium connecting all floors.
  - Fire exits and stairwells on both ends of the building.
  - Accessible ramps and elevators.
  - Green walls integrated into the design.
